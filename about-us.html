Our mission is to make it possible for elementary-school students to learn about the STEM feild through Hands-On Activities after a pandemic which has made kids shift to a less hands-on form of learning.

Rohan Tyagi

President

Hi! I'm a high-schooler focused on engineering/architecture. I have a passion of helping others and want to help others do great things as well! I believe the experiences I had in elementary school shaped how I think about the STEM field today, so I want to help others gain access to the same resources/hands-on activities I had!

Samarth Das

Vice President of Operations

Hi! My name is Samarth Das. I am a freshman at Enloe Magnet GT/AIG Magnet High School. I am also the Vice President of Operations. Growing up, I have been in love with the concept of technology and how it changes our community every second. I have been blessed with the access to technology and will never forget the first time I started typing a couple words on my old 2013 computer. Unfortunately, not everyone is in the same situation I am in and many don’t have access to basic STEM resources. I have joined Scientail to make an impact in society and create a STEM revolution and change how we think of STEM. This revolution starts with us making STEM more accessible than ever.

Alekhya Sadhu

Vice President of Project Management

Hi there! I'm essentially the event planner of the group. I've always loved trying out different mediums in art. Merging this with another interest of mine, STEM, is something I look forward to delving into and presenting to kids as an activity they can really interact with.
