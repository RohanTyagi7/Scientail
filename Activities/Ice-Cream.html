    • Ice Cream!

    In this event, we will be learning about the state of matter and a simple way to make icecream while making ice cream ourselves!

    In this event, we will learn about the basic three states of matter - their similarities, their differences, and how they interact in today's world.

    This event is aimed for 2nd-6th graders.

    We will require masks for all participants in this event and make sure all materials are sanitized before they are used. We will also do our best to maintain social distancing between participants and the instructors as well during the event.
