Welcome to Scientail

Learn about what Scientail is, Sun, Nov 13 2022

Welcome, world, to this awesome nonprofit dedicated to providing the youth of North Carolina with STEM experiments and opportunities!

What is Scientail? Scientail is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing the youth of the Cary-Morrisville with hands-on learning experiences in the field of Science, Technology, Engineering, Math, and Art! We do this through a combination of in person and online events, to encourage participation in our mission of getting kids the skills they need to pursue stem careers later in life. In-person activities will be organized in picnic shelters in various parks around Cary. Most events will be 1-2 hours long, and centered around either engineering, science, art, math, or technology. Keeping in mind the current situation, class sizes will be small and masking mandatory. Utilizing short lessons, crafting activities, and real-world examples, we aim to pique the youths’ interests and show them what’s possible with a stem career. They can gain exposure to all sorts of fields and engage in tasks that reinforce teamwork and individuality. As with most nonprofits, donations are our main source of funding. All funds go to ensuring the future of Scientail as a nonprofit built on helping elementary schoolers get hands-on experience with the STEM field. You can also volunteer to help out with certain activities throughout the year.
